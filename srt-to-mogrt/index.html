Features

One template per cueEvery subtitle in your SRT becomes a styled caption on the timeline, with its text already filled in and its duration matched to the cue.
Premiere Pro templates workMotion Graphics templates authored in Premiere Pro are not writable through the standard scripting interface. This panel rewrites their text directly, so they just work — alongside templates built in After Effects.
Line breaks you controlSet max characters per line and max lines, and every caption re-wraps to fit your design instead of overflowing the box. Full-width = 1, half-width = 0.5.
MOGRT libraryRegister the templates you use often and pick them from thumbnails with looping video previews.
Choose the target textFor templates with several text fields, select exactly which one receives the caption — labels and other controls stay untouched.
Preview before you commitCheck the count, duration, wrapped result, and warnings first. Preview never modifies your sequence.
Automatic backupYour sequence is duplicated before anything is placed (on by default). Re-applying to the same track replaces old captions cleanly.
Private by designEverything stays on your computer. No account, no telemetry, no network access.

Requirements

  • Adobe Premiere Pro 25.6.6 or later
  • Windows / macOS — tested with Premiere Pro 25.6.6 and 2026 on both
  • The target template must have text that is editable in the Essential Graphics panel (After Effects and Premiere Pro templates are both supported)
  • UI language: English / 日本語 (auto-detected, switchable)

Installation

  1. Unzip the download and locate the .zxp file.
  2. Install it with ZXP/UXP Installer (a free tool — search for "ZXP Installer") by dragging the .zxp onto it.
  3. An "unknown publisher" warning is expected — this product is distributed as a self-signed ZXP. Only install ZXP files obtained from the official distributor.
  4. Restart Premiere Pro, then open Window > Extensions > SRT to MOGRT.

Basic usage

  1. 1. Load SRT — click "Select SRT file…". The cue count and the first five cues are shown. UTF-8 (with or without BOM) and Shift_JIS (CP932) are auto-detected.
  2. 2. MOGRT template & settings — add your templates to the library and pick one. Then set the target text, target video track, max characters per line, and max lines.
  3. 3. Preview → Apply — click Preview to check the count, wrapped result, and warnings. If it looks right, click Apply and every cue is placed at once.

FAQ

Does it work with templates made in Premiere Pro?

Yes. Both After Effects and Premiere Pro templates are supported, as long as the target text is editable in Essential Graphics. Support for templates authored in Premiere Pro is the main thing that sets this panel apart — the standard scripting interface cannot write to them at all.

My template fails to place, with an "Unknown error exception".

This almost always means the template was exported from a newer version of Premiere Pro than the one you are running. A Motion Graphics template carries a project file inside it, and Premiere Pro cannot open a project newer than itself — so the template cannot be placed. This is a limitation of the application, not of this panel. The panel detects this and says so in the log. Re-export the template from the version you are editing in, or use a template built in that version.

Can I undo a placement with Ctrl+Z?

No. This panel cannot undo placement — a limitation of the CEP extension platform. Keep the automatic backup option ON (it is on by default) and restore the backup sequence that was created if you need to go back.

Does it work on macOS?

Yes. Windows and macOS are both supported and have been tested with Premiere Pro 25.6.6 and 2026.

Some captions were not placed after applying.

An automatic check after placement detects and re-places missed cues. Confirm that the count in the completion message matches your timeline.

Some characters show as "□".

The font used by your template has no glyph for those characters (this often happens with emoji). Choose a font that covers them.

Does the panel collect any data?

No. Everything is stored locally. No account, no analytics, no network access.

Can I switch the interface language?

Yes — use the button at the top right of the panel to switch between English and 日本語. Your choice is saved.

I get an "unknown publisher" warning when installing.

Expected — the product is distributed as a self-signed ZXP. Only install ZXP files obtained from the official distributor.
